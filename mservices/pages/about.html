About Us

We are the Electrical Contractor and specialist in provision of services and innovation with integrity

Who We Are

  • M SERVICES COMPANY LIMITED is a private, locally registered Company incorporated under Companies Act of 2002 and was given incorporation No.124958.
  • M Services Company Limited is registered as Electrical Works Contractor Class Five with registration number E5/279/07/2018.
  • It is a dynamic mult-disciplinary company and wholly owned and managed by Tanzanians. Its office located at Goba Dar es Salaam, the firm operates country-wide on locally and externally financed projects.
  • The Company was formed with the purpose of meeting the challenges posed by the Electricity
  • Energy Supply Industry in Tanzania.
  • M Services Company Limited is in the Power Engineering Industry. Its strength is based mainly in its technically broad based key staff with experience in the Power Construction/Installations Industry, Services and Maintenance Industry on both Electrical and Mechanical, etc.
